01 —
Brand DNA
Core personality traits that define every design decision
◈
Intelligent
Every interaction feels smart, purposeful, and considered. AI isn't a gimmick — it's the core.
◇
Premium
Polished surfaces, refined spacing, and meticulous details. Comparable to Framer and Linear.
○
Minimal
Remove everything unnecessary. What remains must be perfect. Clarity over decoration.
△
Confident
Bold typography. Strong hierarchy. Never apologetic. The product knows what it is.
⬡
Modern
2024–2025 aesthetic: dark mode, glass effects, subtle motion. Timeless, never trendy.
◉
Human-centered
Built for real people. Non-technical users feel empowered, not intimidated.
